The diverse cuisine of Philadelphia was shaped largely by the city's mixture of ethnicities and history. There are many different types of people that for generations have called Philadelphia their home, and with that comes the amazing variety of foods that the people of the city have to offer. There are certain foods that over time, have specifically become associated with the city of Philadelphia, the main four being cheesesteaks, hoagies, water ice, and the soft pretzel.

Of course there are many places in Philly where one can find and eat these four foods, however, there are a few places in particular that are specifically known for specializing in and serving authentic Philly cheesesteaks, hoagies, water ice, and soft pretzels. The goal of this website is to give you a guide to some of the most authentic places you can find the genuine foods that are associated with the city of brotherly love.
