Water Ice

Known in other states as “Italian Ice”, Philadelphians have seen their water ice as an iconic summer dessert or sweet treat to cool down during those scorching hot days. Most popular in Philadelphia and surrounding areas, there are two places in particular where people in Philadelphia love to go to get their favorite water ice treat.

Philadelphia Water Ice Factory

Philadelphia Water Ice Factory opened its doors in the summer of 1988 in the Port Richmond section of Philadelphia. The headlines read "you don't have to go to South Philly for great Italian Ice any more!" It was an instant success. Owners Ronald Kaleck and son Robert made all the products themselves, working from early morning to late at night. The Philadelphia Water Ice Factory serves a variety of flavors, ranging from the classic cherry and mango, to warheads super sour apple and pina colada. Located at 8354 State Road in Philly, this authentic philly shop place is a great place to get yourself some refreshing water ice!

John's Water Ice

John’s water ice is a family owned and operated business since 1945. Just as founder John Cardullo hand cranked his water ice over 65 years ago, Anthony Cardullo, the third generation owner, uses only fresh fruit he personally selects and squeezes by hand, and adds only filtered water and sugar. John’s uses no preservatives or additives, giving you the most raw and fresh water ice a business could offer. Not only has John’s Water Ice won many local awards, they have also been featured on the Travel Channel and have served President Obama some cool water ice!
